Youth Baseball Team Bench Protocol

Follow these steps when on the bench during batting:

  • Step 1: Check the batting order. Know the three people in front of you and the person following you in the lineup.
  • Step 2: Move your magnet on the field diagram board to indicate your position for the next inning.
  • Step 3: Batteries 1, 2, and 3 get their batting gear ready.
    • Batter 1 should move to the warm-up circle.
    • Batter 2 should start warming up near the on-deck area.
    • Batter 3 should be ready with their gear, waiting behind the fence, observing the pitches.
  • Step 4: Batter 4 passes the scorebook to Batter 5, then prepares their batting gear and waits on the bench.
  • Step 5: During batting:
    • Batter 1 is in the batterâ€™s box.
    • Batter 2 is in the on-deck circle.
    • Batter 3 is behind the fence, watching and stretching.
    • Batter 4 is at the end of the bench, ready to bat next.
    • Batter 5 records the play outcomes in the scorebook during their turn.
  • Step 6: All other players cheer on the team, without their batting gear on, unless they are the fourth batter in line.
