Fielding Instructions for Outfielders

  • Positioning: Stay alert and ready to move towards the ball whenever it is hit in your direction.
  • Play Strategy: Back up infield plays and be ready to cover large areas quickly to prevent the ball from getting past you.
  • Communication: Call out loudly ("I got it!") to claim fly balls and direct other outfielders to back up or cover bases.
  • Backing Up: Always position yourself to back up other outfielders and infield throws, anticipating where the play will develop.
