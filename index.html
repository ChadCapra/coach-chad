Batting: RAD Technique

  • R - Relax: Be relaxed before the pitch is thrown.
  • A - Always Load: Always step and load, ready for action.
  • D - Drive: Drive the ball with power.

Throwing: Key Points

  • Balance: Maintain balance; don't fall forward or backward.
  • Elbow High: Keep the elbow at or above the shoulder.
  • Follow Through: Complete the motion, aiming at the target with the back leg coming over.

Fielding Fundamentals

  • Move towards the ball, assuming you will catch it.
  • Maintain readiness to cover your base or position.
  • Constant movement is key; always know the first part of the play.

Positioning and Awareness

  • Know where the play goes; be in the right spot.
  • Be aware of runners, outs, and the score.
  • Anticipate possible plays, both when you get the ball and when you don't.

Pitching: Technique and Mindset

  • Focus on the umpire, not the batter. Pitch to contact.
  • From the stretch position, ensure balance and firm throws.
  • Follow through is crucial for accuracy and safety.
